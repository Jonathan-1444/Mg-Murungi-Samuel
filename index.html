My Story

My name is Murungi Samuel, born in Hoima City, Western Uganda. I grew up in the Western Uganda Field of the Seventh-day Adventist Church, where my spiritual journey began at just three years old.

I was nurtured in the Adventurer Club, later advancing to the Pathfinder Club, where I learned the principles of discipline, teamwork, and service — living a faithful life in action.

Through this journey, I have gained valuable experience working with Ambassadors and Young Adults, mentoring and inspiring youth across various church entities within the Uganda Union.
