About Me

My name is Murungi Samuel, born in Hoima City, Western Uganda, at Booma Referral Hospital. I grew up in the Western Uganda Field of the Seventh-day Adventist Church, where my spiritual journey began at just three years old.

I was nurtured in the Adventurer Club, later advancing to the Pathfinder Club, where I learned the principles of discipline, teamwork, and service — living a faithful life in action.

Our family later moved to Fort Portal City for ministry, where I continued to serve for nine years at the SDA Church Kagote as an active Pathfinder and youth leader. When we returned to Hoima — where the field headquarters were established — I continued serving under the mentorship of my parents, Pr. Samuel Rwabuyaga and Mrs. Juliet Rwabuyaga, who deeply inspired my spiritual growth and leadership journey.

Later, I enrolled in the Master Guide program in the Western Uganda Field and graduated, having served alongside many dedicated youth in mission work. My desire to grow further in service led me to the North Buganda Field, where I met new friends and joined the Senior Youth Leadership Training at Bugema University, successfully graduating and joining the Senior Youth Family.

Through this journey, I have gained valuable experience working with Ambassadors and Young Adults, mentoring and inspiring youth across various church entities within the Uganda Union. Today, I celebrate the many friendships and networks I have built across Africa through youth ministry — a testimony of God’s uniting love and purpose.

Spiritual Message

“Service to God begins with a willing heart. When young people surrender their talents to Christ, He uses them to change the world. Every act of kindness, every word of encouragement, and every moment of faithfulness is a seed planted for eternity.” — Master Guide Murungi Samuel
